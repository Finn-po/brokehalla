About Pandex

Original game content, organized in one place.

Pandex is an independent platform for original modding and fan-content projects. The launch version starts with curated Brawlhalla map background previews by Brokehalla, while the long-term goal is to support more creators, more games and cleaner discovery for custom content.

What Pandex does

Pandex presents original creator-made game content with clear previews, creator credits and simple navigation. The current version is focused on image previews before downloads are enabled.

What Pandex accepts

Submissions should be original, rights-cleared and suitable for public display. Creator-made map backgrounds, concepts and modding-related assets are the first target category.

What Pandex protects

The platform is built around manual review, takedown handling and a strict no-third-party-IP rule for submissions. Rights holders can request removal through the Takedown Policy.

Launch roadmap

Phase 1Preview-only map showcase with Brokehalla as the first creator.
Phase 2Creator submissions, manual review and a stronger content pipeline.
Phase 3Downloads, ads and analytics after legal and platform checks.
Phase 4Expansion into more games and creator categories.
