Pandex FAQ

Questions before launch.

This page answers the most important questions for visitors, creators and rights holders while Pandex is still in its early preview phase.

What is Pandex?

Pandex is an independent platform for original game-related fan content. The current launch version starts with original Brawlhalla map background previews by Brokehalla.

Are the maps downloadable right now?

No. The current version shows image previews only. Download functionality can be added later after the review, hosting and content workflow are ready.

Is Pandex official?

No. Pandex is an unofficial fan-made project and is not affiliated with Ubisoft, Blue Mammoth Games or Brawlhalla. Game names and trademarks belong to their respective owners.

Can I submit my own map?

Yes. Creators can submit original, rights-cleared content through the submission guide. Submissions are reviewed manually before being added to the platform.

What content is not accepted?

  • Copied artwork or ripped game assets
  • Anime characters or third-party IP
  • Brand logos, copyrighted characters or protected designs
  • Content where the creator cannot confirm ownership or usage rights

How do rights holders contact Pandex?

Rights holders can use the Takedown Policy page linked in the footer. Pandex can remove disputed content after review.

Will Pandex support more games?

That is the plan. The platform starts narrow with Brawlhalla previews first, then can expand to more games once the content process is stable.

How will Pandex make money?

The planned model is advertising-supported access. Ads should only be added after the legal pages, cookie setup and traffic concept are ready.
