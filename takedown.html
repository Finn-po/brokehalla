Takedown Policy

Launch draft · April 2026

1. Rights Holder Requests

If you believe that content on Pandex infringes your rights, contact us at kontakt.pandapages@gmail.com.

2. Required Information

To process a takedown request, include the following information:

  • Your name and contact information.
  • A clear description of the protected work or right.
  • The exact Pandex URL or content name concerned.
  • A short explanation of why you believe the content infringes your rights.
  • A statement that the information you provide is accurate.

3. Review Process

Pandex will review takedown requests manually and may remove or disable access to reported content while the request is being checked.

4. Creator Response

If necessary, Pandex may contact the creator for clarification or proof of rights before deciding whether content remains online.

5. Repeat or Clear Violations

If content appears clearly infringing or a creator repeatedly submits problematic content, Pandex may remove the content and restrict future submissions from that creator.

6. Contact Format

Takedown requests should be sent with the subject Pandex Takedown Request to kontakt.pandapages@gmail.com.
